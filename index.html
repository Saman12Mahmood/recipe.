Kitchen Café Techniques

A Complete Journey Through 12 Topics

Presented by Saman Mahmood

White Chicken Stock

Procedure: Simmer 2 kg chicken bones with 3 L water, mirepoix (125 g onion, 65 g celery, 30 g carrot, 30 g mushrooms), sachet (bay leaf, thyme, peppercorns, parsley, garlic, clove) for 3-4 hours, strain.

Key Ingredient: Chicken Bones - Southeast Asia, 6,000 BC - Collagen for richness

Future Use: Chicken noodle soup

Learning Outcome: Mastered knife cuts and skimming.

Brown Veal Stock

Procedure: Roast 2 kg veal bones at 400°F, simmer with 3 L water, mirepoix (125 g onion, 62.5 g carrot, 62.5 g celery), 31.25 g tomato paste, sachet for 6-8 hours, strain.

Key Ingredient: Veal Bones - Europe, Roman times - Gelatin for silkiness

Future Use: Veal demi-glace

Learning Outcome: Learned roasting for depth.

Vegetable Stock

Procedure: Sweat mirepoix (125 g onion, 62.5 g carrots, 62.5 g celery), 62.5 g leeks, 31.25 g mushrooms, garlic in 11.25 ml oil, simmer with 1 L water and sachet for 45 minutes, strain.

Key Ingredient: Leeks - Mediterranean, ancient Egypt - Sweet balance

Future Use: Leek tart

Learning Outcome: Got sweating veggies.

Béchamel & Mornay Sauce

Procedure: Cook 65 g clarified butter with 65 g flour, add 1 L milk, simmer with bay leaves and clove-studded onion, strain; add 125 g Gruyère, 60 g Parmesan for Mornay.

Key Ingredient: Clarified Butter - India, 1500 BCE - Nutty roux base

Future Use: Shrimp sauté

Learning Outcome: Perfected white roux.

Sauce Espagnole

Procedure: Sauté mirepoix (125 g onions, 65 g carrots, 65 g celery) in 65 g butter, add 65 g flour for brown roux, mix in 1.5 L veal stock, 65 g tomato paste, sachet, simmer 2 hours, strain.

Key Ingredient: Tomato Paste - Italy, 16th century - Umami depth

Future Use: Tomato braised chicken

Learning Outcome: Built brown sauce.

Velouté Sauce

Procedure: Sweat 65 g white mirepoix in 65 g clarified butter, add 65 g flour for blonde roux, mix in 1.25 L white stock, sachet, simmer 1 hour, strain.

Key Ingredient: White Stock - Medieval Europe - Light base

Future Use: Chicken velouté soup

Learning Outcome: Kept it light with blonde roux.

Tomato Sauce

Procedure: Sweat 60 g onion in 60 ml olive oil, add 2 garlic cloves, 1 L canned tomatoes, simmer 10-15 minutes, finish with 10 basil leaves, bay leaf removed.

Key Ingredient: Basil - India, 4,000 years ago - Fresh lift

Future Use: Basil pesto

Learning Outcome: Balanced fresh sauce.

Eggs Benedict with Hollandaise

Procedure: Poach 4 eggs, toast 2 muffins with 45 g butter, top with Canadian bacon, Hollandaise (500 g butter, 5 egg yolks, vinegar reduction, lemon, Tabasco).

Key Ingredient: Eggs - Southeast Asia, 6,000 BC - Rich emulsion

Future Use: Veggie frittata

Learning Outcome: Nailed poaching and Hollandaise.

Goat Cheese & Leek Omelet

Procedure: Sauté 1 leek in 15 ml oil and 15 g butter, add 4 whisked eggs, cook until set, top with 100 g goat cheese, fold.

Key Ingredient: Goat Cheese - Middle East, 10,000 BC - Tangy creaminess

Future Use: Goat cheese salad

Learning Outcome: Learned omelet folding.

Cream of Mushroom Soup

Procedure: Sweat 250 g mushrooms, 100 g shallots, 50 g garlic in 100 g butter, add 70 g flour roux, 1.125 L stock, purée, finish with 200 ml cream.

Key Ingredient: Mushrooms - Europe/Asia, 17th century - Earthy umami

Future Use: Mushroom risotto

Learning Outcome: Mastered creamy soup.

French Onion Soup Gratinée

Procedure: Caramelize 500 g onions in 25 g butter, add 1.3 L beef stock, 35 ml port, simmer, top with baguette and 150 g Gruyère, broil.

Key Ingredient: Onions - Central Asia, 3,000 BC - Sweet depth

Future Use: Onion jam

Learning Outcome: Slow caramelization.

New England Clam Chowder

Procedure: Render 60 g bacon, sweat 100 g onion, 50 g celery, add 25 g flour roux, 400 ml clam juice, 200 g potatoes, finish with 500 ml milk, 50 ml cream.

Key Ingredient: Clams - North America, Native diets - Briny flavor

Future Use: Clam linguine

Learning Outcome: Balanced seafood chowder.

Beef Consommé

Procedure: Mix 250 g beef, mirepoix, 125 g egg whites, simmer with 2.5 L stock until raft forms, strain, garnish with julienne veggies.

Key Ingredient: Egg Whites - Global, 6,000 BC - Clarification

Future Use: Meringue

Learning Outcome: Clarified consommé.

Caesar Salad Dressing

Procedure: Whisk 3 egg yolks with 10 ml Dijon, emulsify with 200 ml olive oil, add 10 g anchovies, garlic, Tabasco, Parmesan.

Key Ingredient: Anchovy Fillets - Mediterranean, Roman times - Salty umami

Future Use: Anchovy butter

Learning Outcome: Emulsified dressing.
